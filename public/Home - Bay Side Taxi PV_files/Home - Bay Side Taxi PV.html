    FAQs

    How to book a boat?

    – WhatsApp
    Send a message to our whatsapp number +52 322 108 0001 and our sales represeentant will follow up your request instantly. This is the best way to book your trip!
    – Phone Call
    Just give us a phone call and we will provide you with all the information to reserve your trip.
    In all cases you will be requested to make a deposit to confirm your trip and the rest will be requested at the moment of the pick up

    Can we arrange the boat for a private tour?

    Absolutely, you can rent the boat to have a private tour with your friends or family; we offer several water experiences like beach clubbing and exploring, snorkeling, whale watching, sunset watching, fishing, etc, so you can choose the one that fit your needs.

    What is the maximum passenger capacity of the water transportation service?

    Our boat can accommodate groups up to 10 people

    What if the weather gets unfavorable for boating?

    We will notify on all our communication channels (Website, Instagram, Facebook) like no service today due to bad weather conditions.
